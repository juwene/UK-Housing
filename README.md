## 📌 Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes UK housing market trends across regions and property types. The project provides insights into property prices, sales performance, regional housing activity, and year-over-year market growth.

The dashboard enables users to explore housing market performance through interactive filters and visualizations, helping identify high-value regions, market trends, and investment opportunities.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions

This dashboard helps answer key business questions:

- Which UK regions have the highest property prices?
- Which regions generate the most housing sales?
- How have property prices changed over time?
- Which property types are appreciating the fastest?
- What is the year-over-year growth of property prices?
- Which property types command the highest average prices?

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Kensington and Chelsea consistently ranks among the highest-priced housing markets.
- Detached properties have the highest average selling prices.
- Property prices have shown strong long-term growth across all property types.
- Regional housing markets exhibit significant differences in both price and sales volume.
- Year-over-year price growth highlights areas experiencing the strongest market appreciation.
